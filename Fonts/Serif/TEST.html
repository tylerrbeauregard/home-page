Consider a square of integer side lengthn. The Mon-drian Art Puzzle requires us to arrange non congru-ent rectangles of integer side length to compose sucha square. The configuration of these rectangles, whencolored, resemble the works of painter Piet Mondrian,hence the name of the puzzle. The defect of the con-figuration is described as the difference between theareas of the largest and smallest rectangles.
